Purpose

The main purpose of this portfolio website is to showcase the different projects I have completed in web design and development. This site will serve as a comprehensive presentation of my skills, creativity, and professional growth in the field, aiming to attract potential clients, employers, and collaborators.

Audience

The target audience for this portfolio website includes potential clients looking for web design and development services, employers seeking to hire a skilled web developer, peers in the web development community who may be interested in collaborating or providing feedback, and students who can learn from the showcased projects and gain inspiration.

Welcome to My Personal Portfolio Website!

Here, I aim to provide you with a comprehensive overview of who I am, what I do, and how I can add value to potential collaborations or projects.

Why Will People Come to My Site?

You're likely here to explore my skills, projects, and experiences in detail. Whether you're a recruiter evaluating my suitability for a job, seeking inspiration, or exploring collaboration opportunities, this site serves as a gateway to understanding my capabilities and achievements.

What Questions Will You Expect My Site to Answer for You?

  • What are my core skills and competencies?
  • What projects have I worked on and what were the outcomes?
  • How can you contact me for potential opportunities?
  • What are my professional achievements and experiences?

What Types of Activities Will You Want to Complete on My Site?

  • View my portfolio to see examples of my work and understand my approach to projects.
  • Read about my background and professional journey to get a sense of my career trajectory and interests.
  • Contact me directly for collaborations or job opportunities, facilitated through a user-friendly contact form.

Portfolio Showcase

A detailed gallery of my projects with descriptions, outcomes, and technologies used will be prominently featured on this website. Each project highlights specific challenges addressed, solutions implemented, and measurable results achieved. This showcase demonstrates my proficiency in JavaScript, HTML/CSS, WordPress, Java, C++, Python, PHP, MySQL, SQL, .NET, Node.Js, Express, Angular, MongoDB, and other technologies, showcasing my ability to deliver impactful outcomes.

About Me Section

In the "About Me" section, you'll find comprehensive information about my professional journey, core skills, achievements, and qualifications. I began my career in the computer science field and have since honed expertise in various areas including software engineering. I hold a high school diploma, an associate's degree in Computer Science from BYU-Idaho (2019), and I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Web Development with a minor in Software Engineering, expected to graduate in June 2024.

I have completed coursework in a variety of computer science fields including Object Oriented Programming, Web Full Stack Development, IT Management and DevOps, User Interface Evaluation and Design, and more. I have a strong proficiency in JavaScript, HTML/CSS, WordPress, Java, C++, Python, PHP, MySQL, SQL, .NET, Node.Js, Express, Angular, MongoDB, and other technologies, leveraging these skills to design and optimize various computer systems.

Outside of academics, I've achieved the rank of Eagle Scout, demonstrating my dedication and leadership skills. I've also completed a development internship with Bloodworks Northwest in Seattle, Washington, gaining practical experience in the field.

Contact and Connect

This section features a user-friendly contact form for inquiries, collaborations, or hiring queries. Whether you're interested in discussing a potential project or simply want to connect, I welcome the opportunity to explore how we can work together.
